DJI Luggage builds suitcase, travel bag, and private-label luggage programs around practical materials, components, branding, and packing requirements.

OUR FOCUS

We help buyers translate market requirements into factory-ready luggage products with clear material, component, branding, and packing options.

Our team supports ABS, PC, ABS+PC, PP, fabric, hybrid, and travel bag programs, including size planning, trims, wheels, handles, locks, lining, and retail packaging.

What We Can Build

[01]

Hard-Shell Suitcases

ABS, PC, ABS+PC, and PP suitcase programs for carry-on, medium, large, and nested set configurations, with molded shell, color, texture, and trim options.

[02]

Soft-Side And Fabric Luggage

Fabric luggage with practical pocket layouts, zipper construction, reinforcement, lining, trolley systems, wheel assemblies, and retail-ready finishing.

[03]

Travel Bags And Duffels

Travel bags, weekender bags, organizer sets, and lightweight companion products for brands that want a broader travel goods line beyond suitcases.

[04]

Private-Label Sets

Coordinated suitcase sets with logo placement, trim colors, hangtags, inserts, carton marks, barcode labels, and packing rules aligned to retail or distributor needs.
